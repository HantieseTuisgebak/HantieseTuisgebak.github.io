Meet Hantie

Hantie is a home baker based in Witbank / eMalahleni, baking traditional South African treats with love and care. Every koeksister is twisted by hand, every melktert is filled with custard made from scratch, and every cupcake is iced with a smile.

Baking is her love language and she'd be honoured to bake something special for you. Whether you're a local or just passing through, Hantie's kitchen is always ready with something sweet.

— Hantie
